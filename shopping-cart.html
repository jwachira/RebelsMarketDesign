Frequently Asked Questions

How do i pay for my shopping cart or order?

You can use a Debit Card, Credit Card, E-Checks or Money Transfer. We process all the payments through Paypal. Paypal accepts all major credit cards and debit cards. Below are some payment methods accepted by Paypal.

I don't have a Paypal Account!

You don't need a Paypal Account. Paypal will accept your Credit Card or Debit Card as well. When redirected to Paypal page, just click 'I don't have a paypal account' and use your Credit Card or Debit Card.

How much is shipping to my country?

To find out if & how much the seller is charging for shipping & handling please select your country on top of the cart. If your country cannot be selected the seller won't ship to your country. If you think shipping is too pricy feel free to contact seller and ask for better prices...

What method of payment can I use?

RebelsMarket & all of its sellers use PayPal to accept payment for your order. This enables you to pay in all common payment options used in your country (Mastercard, Visa, EC-Card etc.) without creating an account with paypal. If not prompted by default select “pay as guest” after clicking pay

When arriving at paypal checkout the item price turned back to US Dollars ($), is that a problem for me or my bank?

Not at all, the price is the same as you have seen before in your cart. Euro, British Pounds etc is automatically converted to US Dollars for RebelsMarket to process and can easily be handled by your credit card company or bank. No need to worry...

Do I have to use Paypal?

Yes & No. Paypal is the only supported payment option at this time but you dont have to use “Login to paypal” if you dont have an account already. If you are not prompted to “Pay as guest” select this option & select your country to get a list of all payment options used in your country!

What if I don't receive my order?

First, contact the store directly. Use the contact link on the seller's booth/storefront to inquire about your order. If you don't receive word back feel free to contact us at support@rebelsmarket.com
